MOTION MOVIE
ROLE OF SIX

Kuleshov Effect
The power of juxtaposition — two images placed side by side create an emotion neither could alone. The foundation of all meaningful editing.
Montage Theory
Eisenstein's collision of ideas — using the cut itself as an expressive instrument to build intellectual tension and visceral rhythm.
Role of Six
Walter Murch's six criteria for the perfect cut: emotion, story, rhythm, eye-trace, two-dimensional plane, and spatial continuity — in that order.
